# Elite Aluminium Completes High-Spec Commercial Shopfront Installation in Braamfontein, Johannesburg

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
**Johannesburg, Gauteng — July 2025**

---

Elite Aluminium, one of Gauteng's most trusted aluminium and glass contractors, has successfully completed a full-floor commercial shopfront glazing project at a prominent commercial property in **Braamfontein, Johannesburg**. The installation showcases the company's ability to deliver precision, large-scale aluminium and glass solutions for the commercial property sector across the East Rand and broader Gauteng region.

## Project Overview

The project involved the design and installation of a complete **floor-to-ceiling aluminium shopfront system** spanning the entire width of a commercial office floor. The installation features:

- **Full-height black aluminium framing** running the full length of the building's interior partition wall
- **Double-leaf glass entrance doors** with brushed brass lever handles — a refined contrast against the dark anodised frames
- **Large fixed glass panels** on either side of the entrance, providing an open, light-filled office environment
- **Structural aluminium curtain wall sections** integrated into the right-wing wall for maximum natural light penetration
- Yellow safety markers applied to all glass panels in compliance with building safety standards

The result is a sleek, modern commercial interior that separates office suites while maintaining full visual openness — a design increasingly favoured by corporate tenants and building developers in Gauteng's commercial property market.

## Why Commercial Clients Choose Aluminium Shopfronts

Aluminium shopfronts and interior glazing systems have become the preferred choice for commercial developers, property managers, and business owners across Johannesburg and Pretoria for several reasons:

**Durability** — Aluminium does not rust, warp, or corrode, making it ideal for high-traffic commercial environments where low maintenance is essential.

**Aesthetics** — Powder-coated and anodised finishes, such as the matte black used in this Johannesburg installation, offer a contemporary look that complements modern office design trends.

**Security** — When paired with toughened or laminated safety glass, aluminium shopfront systems provide robust physical security without sacrificing visual appeal.

**Energy Efficiency** — Large glazed surfaces increase natural light penetration, reducing artificial lighting costs — a significant operational saving for commercial tenants.
