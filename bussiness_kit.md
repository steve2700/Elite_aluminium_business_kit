## 4. Specialist Business Offerings

### 1. Aluminium Windows & Doors — Precision Fabricated to Fit
We manufacture and install aluminium windows and doors custom-sized to your exact openings. Every frame is powder-coated for a clean, lasting finish that resists rust, warping, and weather — built for the Gauteng climate.

### 2. Shopfronts & Commercial Glazing — First Impressions That Last
From retail stores to office parks, we design and install full aluminium-framed glass shopfronts and partitioning systems that look professional and stand up to daily commercial use. We work directly with developers, contractors, and business owners.

### 3. Security Gates & Burglar Bars — Protection Without Compromising Style
We supply and install a full range of security gates and burglar bars for residential estates, complexes, and commercial properties across Gauteng. Strong, estate-compliant, and finished to match your property's aesthetic.

### 4. Sliding, Stacking & Folding Doors — Seamless Indoor-Outdoor Living
Our sliding and stacking door systems are built for smooth operation and maximum glass exposure. Ideal for patios, entertainment areas, and open-plan spaces — we install systems that open up your home without sacrificing security.

### 5. Glass Balustrades & Aluminium Fencing — Clean Lines, Premium Finish
We install frameless and semi-frameless glass balustrades for staircases, balconies, and pool areas, alongside aluminium fencing for perimeter security. Elegant, low-maintenance, and built to last.

### 6. Fast Turnaround & On-Site Quotations — No Guesswork, No Delays
We come to you. Our team assesses the job on-site, provides a detailed written quote, and delivers on agreed timelines. No call centres, no middlemen — you deal directly with the team doing the work, from first call to final installation.
